Welcome to SyncChain

A visual and interactive simulator for blockchain-based supply chain networks. Navigate through dashboards, monitor transactions, and observe live blockchain behavior.

About the Project

SyncChain is designed to simulate a transparent and secure supply chain using blockchain technology. It demonstrates how products flow between different nodes, how blocks are created, and how metrics like cost, time, and efficiency are tracked.

With live network visualization and dynamic metrics, it offers insight into decentralized logistics.
